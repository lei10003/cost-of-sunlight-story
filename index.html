Lede Project 3

The Cost of Sunlight

By: Ligea Alexander

NOTE: This vertical exploration is best experienced on a tablet or desktop screen. Please switch to a larger device for the full experience.

In this guided scroll-through experience, I shed light on the intersections between apartment sunlight exposure, rent prices, and affordability within the vibrant neighborhoods surrounding Prospect Park, Brooklyn. Experience urban living under a new lens, where the sought-after commodity of sunlight takes center stage. See how sunlight exposure and rent intersect with the economic realities of NYC residents.

Are you ready to scale the heights of sunlight economics?
